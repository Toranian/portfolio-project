What makes my plants special?


Well, to be frank, I can't believe you would ask such a question. When I'm not studying or programming, I'm a full time plant Dad. My plants are responsible for good programming and solid studying sessions, without these little guys, I wouldn't be where I am today. My plants are so important that I decided to give them an entire page.

Mother of all Spider Plants


This is my mother spider plant. It's a plant that I got as an itty bitty seedling from a friend when I was in the 11th grade. Since then, she has given birth to about 30 smaller baby plants. As her name implies, she is the mother of all my other spider plants. I'm very proud of her!

Baby Spider Plant


This spider plant is a baby of the mother spider plant shown above. It's grown really nicely and has two babies of it's own on the way!

Pothos


Don't tell the other plants, but this pothos is secretly my favourite. THe colours are fantastic, both white and green on the leaves. It looks as if marble decided to be a plant instead of a rock.
